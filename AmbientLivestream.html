Max MSP Ambient Livestreams
This is a series of livestreams I did on Youtube using a set of Max patches to create improvised ambient soundscapes. The patches were featured on Cycling 74's website here made available for download so other Max users could experiment with them. My motivation behind this project was to bring the process of music making I use directly to listeners and music makers. I created visuals using TouchDesigner for each weak to establish a background or foreground listening and viewing experience.
