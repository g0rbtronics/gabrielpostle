nowhere
Nowhere was a generative 16-channel sound installation presented at the MDW Art Fair, hosted by Mana Contemporary. I developed a custom Max MSP patch that generated 16 independent synth tones, each shifting gradually in volume, tone, and pitch according to whole number ratios. Each voice moved through the 16 different speakers arranged so that the listener may navigate to different zones of the harmonies. The system evolved subtly over the span of the day, making each listening experience unique.
